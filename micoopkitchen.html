MI Co-op Kitchen

The Michigan Electric Cooperative Association (MECA) is the legislative and communicative helper to the electric cooperatives across Michigan. The organization produces legal documents, member campaigns, and a magazine to help update and stay in touch with cooperative members across the state. It also manages several websites, some of which are for cooperatives, and others for different groups within the state's cooperative community.

One such site was MI Co-op Kitchen, a recipe website which allows users to submit their recipes to the state-wide magazine without mailing in a recipe submission, as was previously the process.

When I began working at MECA, one of the first projects I was asked to take over was the management of the MI Co-op Kitchen Wordpress site. I'll admit that this was a new experience for me, as I had never worked with Wordpress before, and had just begun learning about web authoring.

Over the course of my internship with MECA, I was able to learn not only about how posts, pages, and media function in Wordpress, but also about creating portfolio pieces, widgets, and using plugins. This knowledge influenced the my successful management of the MI Co-op Kitchen site, and allowed me to make small but effective changes and updates which impressed the board that my supervisor ultimately presented the project to.

Although the site is now in different hands, many of my contributions, such as the design of the revolution sliders and the user experience of the sign up page, are still online today.
